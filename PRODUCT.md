# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective partners, collaborators, operators, and hires evaluating Resonance Ventures — plus the occasional press or curious visitor arriving from Drop or word of mouth. They come to answer one question: *is this studio serious and worth talking to?* Context is evaluative and unhurried; they read, they judge taste and substance, and the conversion is a single considered action — an email. There is no app, no signup flow, no data capture; the page is a credibility surface.

## Product Purpose

The brand landing for **Resonance Ventures LLC**, a Colorado venture studio that builds and operates software and live experiences (Drop among them). It exists to establish the studio as thoughtful and capable — "we build and operate things that resonate" — across its three frequencies (Software, Music & Events, Operating), and to convert interest into an email conversation. Success = the right people reach out, and the page's restraint and craft do the vouching. Static, no-build, deployed to `resonance-ventures.com` via GitHub Pages.

## Brand Personality

**Resonant · considered · warm-analog.** Quiet confidence over hype. The voice is plain and exact, never salesy. Identity is carried by a single warm gold accent (`--gold: #E7B252`) on deep ink, the standing-wave canvas signature, and a serif/grotesque type system (Bricolage Grotesque + Hanken Grotesk + Space Mono). The wave is the throughline — physics-of-resonance as metaphor for things that carry. Warmth comes from the gold, the copy, and the spacing, not from decoration.

## Anti-references

This should NOT look like:
- **Generic SaaS / template** — agency-template heroes, identical feature-card triplets, eyebrow-on-every-section scaffolding.
- **Corporate / navy fintech** — cold enterprise navy-and-gray, boilerplate "we deliver value" voice.
- **Loud crypto / web3 / VC hype** — neon gradients, "we're disrupting X," aggressive motion. A studio with taste whispers.
- **Linktree-default blandness** — no off-the-shelf page-builder feel; every element should look authored.

## Design Principles

- **Restraint as confidence.** One accent, generous space, exact words. The absence of noise is the message.
- **Signal over noise.** Say less, mean more. Cut any section that doesn't earn its place.
- **The wave is the throughline.** The resonance curve is the one signature; let it carry identity rather than adding ornament elsewhere.
- **Show the work, don't claim it.** Credibility comes from concrete things built and operated (Drop, live events), not adjectives.
- **Performance is taste.** No-build, fast, lean. The lightness is part of the brand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target **WCAG 2.2 AA**. Watch items: the muted grays (`--muted: #8A91A0`, `--muted-2: #5E6473` on `--ink: #08090C`) — verify body text hits ≥4.5:1 and reserve `--muted-2` for large/decorative text only. Provide a `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative for the canvas animation and `.reveal` scroll fades (render content visible by default; don't gate it on a transition). Add visible `:focus-visible` outlines — currently sparse. Decorative SVG/canvas correctly carries `aria-hidden`.
